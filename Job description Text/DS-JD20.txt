SPA has an immediate need for a Data Scientist who can work on high-visibility or mission critical aspects of programs, perform all functional duties independently, and oversee the efforts of less senior staff and/or be responsible for the efforts of all staff assigned to a specific job.

Responsibilities

The successful candidate will be expected to apply quantitative and qualitative methods to solve complex data and analysis problems in a dynamic environment. Likes to be a part of a team and supports all team members – both contractors and government – to achieve the best results. Thinks about solutions and is proactive with recommendations. Provides feedback to improve the process he/she is a part of and seeks help to improve his/herself. Flexible to work both on a government site when necessary as well as in SPA facilities.

Qualifications

Required Qualifications:
Bachelor’s degree in math, physical science, data science, analytics or related STEM field
Experience conducting data analysis and assessment and presenting results
Experience in assisting with the development of professional reports
US Citizenship is required, with ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance and DHS Suitability
Desired Qualifications
DHS Suitability and Secret Clearance
Expertise with at least one coding language like R, Python, VBA or database management system like Neo4j
Previous experience working with a government agency
Experience using methodologies that support technology and investment decisions to improve national security mission success
