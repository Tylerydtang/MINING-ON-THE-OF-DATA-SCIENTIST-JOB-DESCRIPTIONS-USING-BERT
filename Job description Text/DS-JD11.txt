Data Science @ Pave

Thousands of companies trust our market data to make compensation decisions that impact the livelihood of employees across the globe. Our Data Science team drives the accuracy, rigor, and insights that fuel our mission to build the world's best compensation tools and easily accessible market data so companies can plan, communicate, and benchmark in real-time.

Where you will focus:

Helping to define processes, platforms, and tools that will scale with Pave's exponential growth.
